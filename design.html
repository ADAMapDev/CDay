Design

During the design phase of the SDLC, the team heavily considered those with color vision deficiency. Although high-contrast mode was a Won’t have feature due to time constraints, we still wanted the UI to appear as accessible as possible. Therefore, AIM’s color scheme consists of a monochromatic, high-contrast design for easy element distinguishing.

When displaying elevation changes, the shifts between two instructions are denoted using starkly different colors:


  • Red: 1.5+
  • Yellow:​ 0.5 - 1.4m
  • Green: 0.1 - 0.4m
  • Teal: less than 0m

Additionally, AIM prioritizes visual cues:


  • Icons and text on buttons​​
  • Underlining links on hover​
  • Button color shifts on hover/click​​
